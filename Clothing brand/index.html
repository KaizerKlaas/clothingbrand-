Brand Definition

The name KUBOOOO is derived from the XHOSA chant "KUBO" which means "Attack" or "Upon them" not neccesarily referring to violance but has to "attack" their dreams and goals.

The brand is a reminder to those who are wearing it to always chase thier dreams and goals. It's a refletion that anything is possible as long as you believe.
